T-Shirt Concept: Bearcubbin'!

Bearcubbin'! was a mostly instrumental, usually heavy, and always impressive math-rock trio active throughout the U.S. from 2009 to 2016. I was fortunate enough to brush paths with these guys several times and ended up provided a t-shirt design for their 2014 tour in support of the album "Girls with Fun Haircuts."

Sadly, Bearcubbin'! recently announced their conclusion as a band. But you can still enjoy their records, and you can still grab a shirt.

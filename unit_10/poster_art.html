Poster Art: LORD

For the several years that they were active, I handled design work for the band LORD, a loud instrumental trio based out of Portland, Oregon. In addition to shirts and album art, producing show fliers was a common exercise. It was also a fun way to integrate interesting images and themes into the band's otherwise minimalistic and at times obscure visual identity.

The selection here represents a mix of photography, illustration, found art, collage, and digital painting.

While no announcement was ever issued by the band regarding their continuation, their last performance was in December 2013. They've'remained quiet since, but you can hear them here and here.

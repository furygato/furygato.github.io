Logo: Grst

Grst is a black metal band whose output is thematically centered around humankind's destructive predisposition, particularly with regard to the natural world. They are also a band which forgoes engagement with many of the standard black metal tropes. For their logo, I rendered an antiquated letter "G" as a violent, organic shape that testifies to the primal struggle between order and chaos.

The rune also purposefully offends any sense of symmetry. This choice was made both because symmetry seemed not to belong within the statement that Grst makes on the whole, and also because it was a way to further detach the band's visual identity from the conventions used by more traditional black metal artists.

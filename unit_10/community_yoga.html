Logo: Community Yoga Project

Community Yoga Project brings yoga and meditation to people wherever they are: the workplace, the classroom, group retreats, parties, private homes, etc. Sessions are led by a certified instructor, and often used as a way to raise funds for various local efforts.

Many of the graphics associated with yoga are very similar (a quick look around the web reveals lots and lots of lotuses...) so for this project I worked to avoid overused imagery and emphasize what is unique about Community Yoga Project: the focus on people working together for good. The logo features subtle figures in active pose and arranged in a mandala pattern, at once communicating the underlying physical and spiritual aspects of yoga, as well as the communal approach of this particular practice.
